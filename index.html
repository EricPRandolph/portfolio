About Me

I am a fourth-year Mechanical Engineering student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute committed to developing my professional and technical skills within a multidisciplinary environment, particularly in the fields of Defense and Additive Manufacturing. As an engineer, I prioritize teamwork and communication, integrating expertise and soft skills to achieve impactful solutions that benefit the stakeholders.

I am strongly self-motivated to teach myself the skills needed for personal engineering projects that I have taken on in my free time, and I especially enjoy projects with a competitive goal. This year marks my 9th year in competitive robotics across the FIRST Tech Challenge, VEX U Robotics Competition, and Combat Robotics. Consequently, many of my engineering projects have revolved around team environments. This has allowed me to develop my soft skills in communication, time management, professional responsibility, and conflict management.

I am particularly interested in exploring the use of additive manufacturing throughout the engineering design process from end to end, having employed it in many of my own projects for rapid prototyping and, in many cases, final design solutions.
