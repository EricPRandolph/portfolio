Absolute Zero

Subtitle

Objectives

  • Develop a 3-lb combat robot for the National Havoc Robot League competition
  • Design and manufacture robust and lightweight robotic components out of advanced FDM materials such as glass-filled nylon and TPU
  • Create a weapon system capable of consistently storing and delivering 600J of energy without major structural failures

Outcomes

  • Successfully competed at numerous events with a total record of 6-4
  • Developed modular robot components (weapon assembly, wiring harness, etc.) to decrease maintenance time between fights, allowing for hot-swappable components
  • Employed additive manufacturing to design and assemble a weapon assembly without the use of a machined aluminum hub, decreasing overall cost and assembly time

Skills

  • Inventor/Onshape CAD
  • Mechanical Design
  • CNC Machining and Shop Tools
  • Soldering and Brushless Motor Control
  • 3D-Printing (FDM)
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Design for Manufacturing & Assembly

Flashfreeze

Subtitle

Objectives

  • Create a 3-lb combat robot for the National Havoc Robot League competition
  • Develop multi-body part modeling skills to allow armor package to smoothly transition shape across the robot
  • Use a parametric master sketching approach to quickly iterate through designs
  • Implement a new wiring harness focused on absorbing and dissipating voltage spikes without damaging electronic speed controllers or related components

Outcomes

  • Implemented transient-voltage-suppression (TVS) diodes in tandem with additional capacitors to protect the electronic components from voltage spikes during weapon collisions
  • Developed a 3D-printed modular armor package using multi-body part modeling, allowing for various sections to be replaced independently
  • Optimized weapon blade geometry using Fusion 360 FEA to simulate out-of-plane weapon impacts

Skills

  • Inventor/Onshape CAD
  • Electrical Design
  • Finite-Element Analysis
  • CNC Machining and Shop Tools
  • Soldering and Brushless Motor Control
  • 3D-Printing (FDM)
